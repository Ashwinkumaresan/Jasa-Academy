About

We are a group of college students dedicated to making a positive impact in our community. To support our cause, we organize various indoor competitions and events. The funds raised from these activities are donated directly to orphanages, helping to provide essential resources and support to children in need. By combining our passion for competition with our commitment to social responsibility, we aim to make a meaningful difference in the lives of these children.

Join us in our efforts to create a brighter future for those who need it most.
